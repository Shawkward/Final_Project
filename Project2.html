Kevin Shaw

Graphic Designer + Web Developer

In November I completed a 13 month contract with ClubLink where I had been the Graphic Design Manager. While with ClubLink I was responsible for creating ad campaigns, brochures, menus, online and social media graphics and all of the graphic design needs of the 50+ golf clubs, wedding and corporate event venues across Ontario, Quebec and Florida. Prior to my position with ClubLink, for six years I was the Art Director of Clean Eating magazine. There I was responsible for the overall look and feel of the Clean Eating Brand, over a spectrum of communication disciplines with a focus on print, web, social media, video and mobile.
